An industrial platform for biogas and biomethane.

Identity

What is FAR.E

FAR.E is an industrial platform dedicated to the management of biogas and biomethane plants.

We work with investors, owners and developers to transform complex plants into governable and high-performing assets.

We were born to bring industrial method to a sector that needs it. Not to do consulting. We work on operating plants, where every day the margin is built or destroyed.

Our job is not to manage plants. It is to make them governable.

The why

Why FAR.E exists

FAR.E brings industrial method to biogas and biomethane. Because the sector was born agricultural, but today it is an energy industry — and in many cases is still managed as it was 20 years ago.

Plants grow in number and complexity, but management remains fragmented, reactive, non-standardized. Value is lost every day without anyone measuring it.

FAR.E exists because biogas is managed like agriculture, but generates value like industry.

Industrial method

Where there is improvisation, we bring structure. Defined processes, traceable decisions, clear roles.

Governance

Where there is reactivity, we bring control. Data read, anomalies anticipated, improvement cycles.

Measurable performance

Where there are only activities, we bring results. Defined KPIs, tracked objectives, created value.
