Services

FAR.E. services are designed to govern the entire plant system, not individual activities.

We intervene on an industrial system. Each component below is part of an integrated model, not an isolated service.

Our goal is to create value by governing the system. Because most plants lose margin every day due to lack of industrial control.

Plant Management

Operational control and industrial plant management

We manage the plant as an industrial asset, not as agricultural infrastructure. Every operational decision is structured, every parameter is monitored, every anomaly is managed before it becomes a problem.

  • Continuous performance monitoring
  • Operational activity coordination
  • Planned and predictive maintenance
  • Anomaly and intervention management
  • Reporting and KPIs

Feedstock & Recipes

Supply chain management and digester diet optimization

We don't buy feedstock — we govern its quality and value. Every contract is based on real energy value, not tonnage. Every recipe is optimized to maximize methane production at the lowest cost.

  • Supplier selection and qualification
  • Input quality control
  • Contract and logistics management
  • Biological recipe optimization
  • Real cost reduction per Nm³ CH₄

Project Development & Origination

Identification, development and structuring of investment opportunities

We don't analyze projects. We identify them, structure them and bring them to maturity. We operate upstream of the industrial cycle, where value is created: in opportunity selection, building the authorization foundations and defining investable assets.

  • Investment opportunity identification
  • Project pipeline development
  • Authorization support
  • Technical and economic structuring
  • Investor alignment

Digital & AI

Decision support and advanced monitoring

We don't start from technology. We start from real plants. Everything we develop comes from concrete problems and serves to make operational decisions faster, informed and traceable.

  • Data collection and integration
  • Performance and anomaly analysis
  • Predictive model development
  • Dashboards and reporting
  • Evolution towards digital twin
